1/*
2 * Function and variable attributes.
3 *
4 * Whether the compiler supports attributes (GCC/Clang), `declspec`s (MSVC) or neither,
5 * this header creates platform-independent macros for certain attributes a function
6 * or variable may have.
7 *
8 * Some combinations may (definitely will) be missing; this isn't an exhaustive list.
9 * (Though all macros are safe to use)
10 *
11 *
12 * Created by MacBook on 10.01.2025.
13 */
